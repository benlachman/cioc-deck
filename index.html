05 // Operational Boundaries

What the Operations Center Does Not Do

Mission creep kills coordination functions. Clear boundaries maintain effectiveness.
  • Does not make grants. Brokers connections. CEF/Renphil remain grantmaking entities.
  • Does not conduct research. ACER, Ocean Visions, practitioners do the science.
  • Does not lobby directly. CCAN and Climate Hub own Hill relationships.
  • Does not deploy interventions. Practitioners deploy. This function ensures they're funded and coordinated.
  • Does not publish reports. Every output is operational — specific introduction, funding match, media placement.
